I'm so glad you are all visiting this page to learn more about giving an animal from the Athens Humane Societya forever home. AHS is a non-profit organization and prides itself on being a no-kill shelter. They rescue over 5,000 animals a year and make it afforable to save the life of a sick animal as bes they can.

Besides that, they have options to adopt, volunteer, or donate.Their service to these animals would not be possible without the help of the people in the community. Adoptions come with a lot of great benefits so I encoruage you to check out the official AHS site.
